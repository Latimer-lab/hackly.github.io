Collective intelligence. Unlocking breakthroughs

Collective intelligence. The crowd can analyze a large amount of knowledge with its workforce. Latimer offers everyone the opportunity to create a solution from scratch or to improve on existing ideas. Thus, even unusual or bizarre approaches can be tested, the most promising ideas can be explored further and the least effective ones eliminated. This method differs from traditional methods that are limited to a competitive model and require participants to write a full answer themselves, which requires at least a large network or expertise in the overall problem they need to solve.
